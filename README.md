# Sales analysis project

**Project Objective**

The goal of this project was to analyze company revenue and profitability across project types, locations, and time in order to identify key financial trends and performance drivers.

**Key insights**

• Total net revenue for the analyzed period reached €4.04M, with ERP Implementationprojects generating the largest share (approx. 55%).

• Warsaw and Wroclaw were the top-performing cities, together accounting for more than half of total revenue.

• Revenue remained relatively stable between 2013 and 2019, followed by a noticeable decline after 2020, indicating a potential shift in market conditions.

• The company achieved total profit of €479K with an overall profit margin of 11.88%.

• Business Intelligence (BI) projects showed the highest profitability (~20% margin), while Training Services generated negative profit, suggesting a need to review cost structure in this category.

• A declining net profit trend has been visible since around 2016, indicating potential efficiency or cost-related challenges that require further investigation.



**Technical highlights**

• Designed interactive dashboards in **Power BI** with KPIs such as Revenue, Costs, Profit, and Profit Margin
• Performed data cleaning and transformation using **Power Query**
• Built calculated measures **in DAX** to support profitability and trend analysis
• Implemented dynamic filtering by project type, industry, and location
